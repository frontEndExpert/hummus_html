Simon's Pattaya Hummus Laboratory

Definitely The Best Hummus In Thailand.

When we set up the restaurant our only goal was (and still) to create the best and most delicious hummus and hummus related dishes around our kitchen (LAB).
We combine between traditional and contemporary recipes - Innovation is the key word.
According to the positive feedback that we are constantly getting we feel that we achieved our mission and we hope that wherever, whenever, you are thinking of hummus you will think of Simon's Pattaya Hummus Laboratory.


All the food at our place is prepared daily and made out of the best fresh ingredients.
We serves mouthwatering dishes from morning to 1AM.
We created a place dedicated to all of us -> Hummus Lovers.
We based our concept on hummus, hummus & more hummus (chickpeas).
